Video Lecturer 9 "How to raise money"

Oct 1, 2017  

Hello everyone! In this video lecture guests are Marc Andreessen, Ron Conwa and Parker Conra. This video lecture is more like a question and answer and honestly I don’t know how to review it. So I will not review this video, but what I will do is write in several points what I got out of this video.

What I learned in this video lecture is:

To get people invest in your company you must show that you are a leader, you are obsessed with your idea and you need the product as well

Bootsrap as long as you can. If it is possible to do the job without raising a money, than that is the better way.

You need to trust your investors. Don’t ask them to sign an NDA at the beginning of the relationship as it will indicate that you don’t trust them.

After an investor made a commitment to you the first thing you have to do is to write an email which confirms what the said to you.

It is important to choose the right investors. If the investors are trusted by others, it will be easier for you to get other people to invest in your startup.

An investor will usually invest in competing companies, so if the investor you try to attract already invested in a competitor company, it is better to find another investor.
